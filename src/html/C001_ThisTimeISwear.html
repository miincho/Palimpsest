THIS TIME I SWEAR.

Wednesday, June 19, 2024.

During our junior year at Parsons School of Design, Mink and I promised to complete a project together. It is currently June, and we are a few sessions in.

To catch you up to speed:

While brainstorming during Meeting #1, we decided that fearlessness and accountability were at the heart of the Palimpsest Passion Project, or PPP. Meeting #2 was about concept refinement and organization. We presented mood boards and discussed how we saw PPP. From there, we planned to have Figma sketches ready for Meeting #3. And now, post-meeting, we have a solid direction. Concerning content, we will contribute “articles,” which can mean anything from finding quotes, writing captions or notes, or sourcing visual references. PPP is an inspiration archive.
